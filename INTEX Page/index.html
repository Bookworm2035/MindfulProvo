Y Desserts

A Provo Original

  • Welcome to Y Desserts

    Your ultimate guide to satisfying your sweet tooth in Provo, Utah right next to the iconic Y on the mountain! As devoted BYU students, we understand the Y is more than just a symbol; it's a daily reminder of our alma mater and the "Why" behind our actions.

  • Inspiration

    Inspired by our love for desserts and the communal spirit of Provo, we've created Y Desserts to help fellow locals navigate the delightful world of sweet treats. Whether you've conquered the Y hike or simply admire it from afar, no judgement, we're here to guide you on a journey of delectable discoveries.

  • Enjoyment

    Life is meant to be savored, and what better way to find joy than through indulging in the finest desserts Provo has to offer? Our curated collection showcases our favorite restaurants and their mouthwatering desserts, ensuring you experience the sweetest side of life.

Share the sweetness with your friends, because together, we can help everyone in Provo find their perfect "Y dessert" moment.

  • Your Y

    Join us on this sugar-fueled exploration, and let's make Provo the sweetest place to satisfy those cravings. Because at Y Desserts, we believe that every dessert has a story, and we're here to help you uncover the "Y" behind each delightful bite.
